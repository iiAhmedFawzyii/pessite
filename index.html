Who we are

We are an Egyptian start -up , specializing in educational technology in the fields of artificial intelligence and computer science, we offer unique, simplified and professional content for children aged 8 -15 years.

Our team from different universities ranging from artificial intelligence to computer science and engineering, and we aim to provide different educational courses in the fields of programming and artificial intelligence to help the future generation build a better future.

VISION

To have a brighter future for the next generation and help them find their passion.

MISSION

To reach as many children as possible and help them grow to be the best and brightest version of themselves. Providing distinctive and interesting content that is suitable for the child’s mentality and develops his intelligence and skills.
